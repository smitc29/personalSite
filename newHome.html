Hey there! My name is Christopher Smith. I'm a graphic designer and software developer, who also loves creating games.

If you're looking for someone who knows about code, I've got field experience to make your project a reality. Algorithms are my specialty!

Or if you need a digital artist or graphic designer, art has been a passion of mine since I was a child; if you're curious, I can send you samples of my work.

Web design comes naturally to me, and I try to make each site visually unique and memorable. I hope this site serves as a good example!

Looking to make a board or video game? Game design fascinated me for years, and I began delving into game engines such as Unity in college.

Need to make something a little more secure? I've delved into cybersecurity, and I'm more than willing to do a little work to take some anxiety off your shoulders.

Of course, these aren't all of my skills. I'm a quick learner with a solid background in teaching others mathematical and code concepts. I hope that I'll be able to demonstrate this in person at some point!
