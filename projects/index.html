Artware Site

This site is a basic sample of a website that could be used for a local business. It features columns that allow it adjust for mobile viewing, and basic colors which can easily be reconfigured for your own personal tastes. Despite its flexible nature and accessibility, this site has no Wordpress or Bootstrap elements, and is entirely made out of code from scratch.

Tutorial Site

As I studied web design, I began experimenting with different ideas for what could work on a website to make it unique. This site is such an experiment; I had the idea to make a website with the navigation hidden just offscreen, which the user can bring onscreen with the click of a button at the top of the screen. While not revolutionary, it did serve to encourage me to try out new mechanics in web design.

Athlete Site

Personal websites are fairly important nowadays, but some athletes, musicians, and entertainers have fan sites or professional websites informing the public of their upcoming events. This site is meant to be a sample of what an athlete's site could potentially look like. I used Hailey Langland, from the Winter 2018 Olympics as the athlete of choice for this site, as I felt like most snowboarders are overshadowed in the Olympics.
