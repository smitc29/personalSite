Job Hunter

Fed up with having to browse multiple job application sites while unemployed, I decided to develop a javascript application that could scrape information from multiple different job search sites. To find relevant positions, please select which platforms you'd like looked at, then fill in the provided forms with data relevant to what you're interested in. Adding a location and key words is necessary to provide accurate results, and will help more than you might think! (Please note that this application is designed to help applicants in the United States, and isn't designed to accomodate other countries at the moment)
